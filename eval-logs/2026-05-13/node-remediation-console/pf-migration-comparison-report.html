🪟 Modal Migration fix-engine

🔀 DragDrop → DragDropSort Migration fix-engine

DragDrop, Droppable, and Draggable are deprecated. The replacement is DragDropSort from the new @patternfly/react-drag-drop package, which uses @dnd-kit under the hood.
